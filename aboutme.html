Student, Dreamer, Activist

My name is Noah Acosta, my pronouns are He/Him/His and it is a pleasure to meet you virtually! I am a Computer Science student at The University of Texas at Dallas in Richardson, Texas. I was born and raised in Mesquite, Texas. I am an aspiring full-stack developer/java developer hoping to make an impact in the world.

In my free time I enjoy playing video games, browsing the web, and tinkering with technology. I label myself a 'Dreamer' because whilst I am a realist, I do believe there is good to be found in this world and dreaming big and having goals pushes a person to be the best they can be.

Aside from technology, I am a activist for LGBTQIA, Womens, and POC rights. I strive to educate and advocate for marginalized voices that are often overlooked in the media. The world is not a perfect place, and there is still work to be done. And I am prepared to work hard to be a beacon of hope for those who feel unheard, unappreciated, and underrepresented.
